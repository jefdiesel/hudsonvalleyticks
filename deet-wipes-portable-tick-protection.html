How to Use DEET Wipes

  1. Open individual wrapper
  2. Wipe exposed skin (arms, legs, neck, ears)
  3. Make sure to wipe like you're cleaning—rub thoroughly
  4. One wipe can cover entire exposed body if done carefully
  5. Wash hands after application (don't touch face/eyes)
  6. Allow to dry before getting dressed
  7. For air travel, fold the open wrapper to keep unused portion moist for later use
💡 Pro Tip: One wipe covers your arms, legs, neck and face if you work carefully. Two wipes is ideal for very thorough coverage. With 24 wipes, you can protect yourself for multiple outdoor trips.

DEET Wipes vs. Spray

  • Coverage: Wipes provide better, more even coverage than spray

  • Mess: No overspray or waste; wipes are targeted
  • Travel: TSA-approved in carry-on luggage
  • Cost per use: Roughly equivalent to spray
  • Convenience: Wipes are easier for solo application (especially face/neck)
