Why Complete Kits Work

Complete protection addresses vulnerabilities that piecemeal approaches miss:

  • Hat: Ticks climb onto scalp and hairline
  • Belt: Covers waist area where pants meet shirt (common attachment point)
  • Leg Gaiters: Stops ticks at ankle level
  • Arm Cuffs: Protects wrists and inner arms
  • Bandana: Protects neck and lower face

Together, these pieces eliminate most tick entry points to your body.
