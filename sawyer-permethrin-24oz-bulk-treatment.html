How to Apply Sawyer Permethrin

  1. Spray outdoors or in well-ventilated area
  2. Hold bottle 6-8 inches from fabric
  3. Apply with slow, sweeping motion
  4. Treat both sides of each garment (front and back)
  5. Treat for 30 seconds per side
  6. Use 4.5 ounces per complete outfit
  7. Allow to dry completely (2-4 hours)
  8. Hang outside to air dry before wearing
⚠️ Important: Don't apply DEET-based repellents over wet permethrin. Apply permethrin first, let dry completely, then apply skin repellent if needed.
